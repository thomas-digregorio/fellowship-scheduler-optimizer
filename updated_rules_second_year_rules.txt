All Second year fellows must complete the following number of rotations 

4 weeks of vacation (ideally divided into 1 two-week vacation and 2 one-week vacation blocks) 

0 weeks of white consults 

1-3 weeks of SRC consults 

1-3 weeks of VA consults 

5-6 weeks of Goodyer consults 

0-2 weeks of CCU  

0-1 weeks of night float 

3-5 weeks of EP 

1-4 weeks of CHF 

4-6 weeks of yale nuclear 

3-4 weeks of VA nuclear 

4-12 weeks of yale echo 

0-2 weeks of VA echo 

2-12 weeks of SRC echo 

3-4 weeks of yale cath 

2-5 weeks of VA cath 

0-4 weeks of SRC cath 

6 weeks of research 

2 weeks of CT-MRI 

CT-MRI must be completed as 2 consecutive weeks 

0 weeks of Elective 

0 weeks of peripheral vascular 

For Second year fellows, the total weeks of SRC consults + VA consults = 3-4 weeks 

For Second year fellows, the total weeks of Yale cath + SRC cath + VA cath = 6-8 weeks 

For Second year fellows, the total weeks of Yale echo + SRC echo + VA echo = 10-12 weeks 

For Second year fellows, the total weeks of Yale nuclear + VA nuclear = 7-10weeks 

For Second year fellows, 1 week of Research should be before or after 1 week vacation blocks if possible 

Second year fellows cannot be on CCU after 8/2/26  

Second year fellows cannot be on Night float after 8/23/26 

Second year fellows ideally should be on research during the week of 7/5/27-7/11/27 (week 52) 

CCU and night float rotations should ideally be evenly distributed between second year fellows 

For second years, research cannot be more the 2 consecutive weeks 

SRC echo ideally should be given to second year fellows
