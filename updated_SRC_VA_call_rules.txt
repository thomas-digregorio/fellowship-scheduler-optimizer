SRC/VA weekend call start:

There is one first year fellow or one second year fellow on SRC/VA weekend call every weekend 

First year fellows can't be given SRC/VA weekend call until March 

SRC VA weekend call can only be given to fellows on the following rotations: 

 EP 

CHF 

 yale nuclear 

 VA nuclear 

yale echo 

VA echo 

SRC echo 

 yale cath 

VA cath 

SRC cath 

CT-MRI 

Fellows cannot be on src/va call and 24 hour call in the same weekend 

Each second year fellow should have 5 - 6 SRC/VA weekend call  

Each first year fellow should have 0-1 SRC/VA weekend call 

For first year fellows, the total number of SRC/VA weekend call + 24 hour call should be = 6-7 

Weekend or weekday call on December 24 and/or December 25 ideally should be given to a fellow who is is on EP, CHF, yale nuclear, yale echo, VA echo, SRC echo, yale cath, VA cath, SRC cath, CT-MRI, research, vacation during Thanksgiving week (November 23-29) 

Weekend or weekday SRC/VA call ideally cannot be given to the same fellow on Thanksgiving (November 26), Christmas eve (December 24) , Christmas Day (December 25), and New Year’s eve (December 31).  ie the same person cannot be on call Christmas Eve and New Year's Eve or thanksgiving and Christmas.

 





SRC/VA weekday call start:

There is one first year or one second year fellow on SRC VA weekday call every weekday night: monday, tuesday, wednesday, thursday 

First year fellows cannot be given SRC/VA weekday call until January 

SRC/VA weekday call can be given only to fellows on the following rotations: 

 yale nuclear 

 VA nuclear 

VA echo 

SRC echo 

 yale cath 

VA cath 

SRC cath 

CT-MRI 

Fellows cannot be on SRC/VA weekday call for more than 1 consecutive night 

Each second year fellow should have between 18-20 SRC/VA weekday night calls 

Each first year fellow should have between 3 – 5 SRC/VA weekday night calls 

Ideally no more than one weekday call per week 

Ideally not on call during a week day if on call VA SRC over that weekend (after)
