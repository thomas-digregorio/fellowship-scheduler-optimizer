Each rotation requires the following number of fellows in each week 

vacation - NA 

white consults- 1 First year Fellow 

SRC consults- 1 First year, Second year, or Third year fellow 

VA consults- 1 First year, Second year, or Third year fellow 

Goodyer consults- 1 Second year fellow 

CCU- 1 Second year fellow from 7/13/26 – 8/2/26, 1 First year fellow beginning 8/3/2026 

Night Float- 1 Second year fellow from 7/13/26 – 8/16/261, 1 First year fellow beginning 8/17/26 

EP- 1-2 First year or Second year Fellows 

CHF- 0-1 First year or Second year Fellow 

Yale nuclear- There should be 1-2 fellows on Yale Nuclear at a time (1st or 2nd year) 

VA nuclear- 1 First year or second year Fellow 

Yale echo- 0-2 First year Fellows and 2-3 Second or Third year Fellows 

VA echo- 1 First year or 1 Second year Fellow 

SRC echo- 1 First year, 1 Second year, or 1 Third year Fellow 

Yale cath- 0-2 First year, Second year, or Third year Fellows 

VA cath- 1 First year or Second year Fellow 

SRC cath- 0-1 First year or Second year fellow 

2 weeks of research- NA 

CT-MRI- 0-1 second year or 0-1 third year fellow 

Elective- 0-9 third year fellows 

Peripheral vascular- 0-1 third year fellow 

There can be a maximum of 4 fellows from the same year with vacation at the same time 

All First year fellows must complete the following number of weeks per rotation 

4 weeks of vacation (ideally divided into 1 two-week vacation and 2 one-week vacation blocks) 

5-6 weeks of white consults 

2-4 weeks of SRC consults 

3-4 weeks of VA consults 

0 weeks of Goodyer consults 

5-6 weeks of CCU beginning 8/3/26 

5-6 weeks of Night Float beginning 8/17/26 

4-5 weeks of EP 

2  weeks of CHF 

2-4 weeks of Yale nuclear 

1-3 weeks of VA nuclear 

3-4 weeks of Yale echo 

3-4 weeks of VA echo 

1 weeks of SRC echo 

3-4 weeks of Yale cath 

1-2 weeks of VA cath 

1-2 weeks of SRC cath 

2 weeks of research 

0 weeks of CT-MRI 

0 weeks of Elective 

0 weeks of peripheral vascular 

For First year fellows, the total weeks of White consults + SRC consults + VA consults = 10-12 weeks 

For First year fellows, the total weeks of Yale cath + SRC cath + VA cath = 6-7 weeks 

For First year fellows, the total weeks of Yale echo + SRC echo + VA echo = 7-9 weeks 

For First year fellows, the total weeks of Yale nuclear + VA nuclear = 5-7 weeks 

For First year fellows, 1 week of Research should be before or after 1 week vacation blocks if possible 

There should be no more than 3 weeks of vacation or research within a 4 week rolling period 

All first year fellows must complete 1 week of yale echo, 1 week of yale or SRC cath, 1 week of white consults, 1 week of CCU, 1 week of EP, and 1 week of CHF before having their first Night Float rotation 

For First year fellows, ideally 1 week of CHF should be immediately before the 1st night float rotation 

Night float cannot come after the following rotations: white consults, SRC consults, VA consults, CCU, vacation  

First year fellows during their first nuclear rotation must be paired with a second year fellow or a first year fellow who has previously completed at least 1 week of yale nuclear 

First year fellows cannot be on CCU before 8/3/26 

First year fellows cannot be on night float before 8/17/26 

First year fellows cannot be on research or vacation before 8/11/26 

The first night float rotation ideally should be 1 week
