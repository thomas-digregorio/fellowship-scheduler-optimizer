The calendar year begins 7/13/2026 and ends 7/11/2027.
Every week begins on a Monday and ends on a Sunday. 
There are 9 First year Fellows, 9 Second year Fellows, and 7 Third year fellows. 
Each weekly rotation requires the following number of fellows in each week at minimum
vacation - NA
white consults- 1 First year Fellow
SRC consults- 1 First year, Second year, or Third year fellow
VA consults- 1 First year, Second year, or Third year fellow
Goodyer consults- 1 Second year fellow
CCU- 1 Second year fellow from 7/13/26 – 8/9/26, 1 First year fellow beginning 8/10/2026
Night Float- 1 Second year fellow from 7/13/26 – 8/16/261 First year fellow
EP- 1 First year or Second year Fellow
CHF- 1 First year or Second year Fellow
Yale nuclear- 1 second year Fellow, with no weekly first year minimum
VA nuclear- 1 First year or 1 second year Fellow
Yale echo- 0-3 First year Fellows and 2-3 Second or Third year Fellows
VA echo- 1 First year or 1 Second year Fellow
SRC echo- 1 First year, 1 Second year, or 1 Third year Fellow
Yale cath- 1-2 First year, Second year, or Third year Fellows
VA cath- 1 First year or Second year Fellow
SRC cath- 1 First year, Second year, or 3rd year Fellow
2 weeks of research- NA
CT-MRI
Elective
There can be a maximum of 4 fellows from the same year with vacation at the same time
All First year fellows must complete the following number of weeks per rotation
4 weeks of vacation (ideally divided into 1 two-week vacation and 2 one-week vacation blocks)
4-6 weeks of white consults
3-4 weeks of SRC consults
3-4 weeks of VA consults
0 weeks of Goodyer consults
5-6 weeks of CCU beginning 8/10/26
5-6 weeks of Night Float beginning 8/17/26
3-5 weeks of EP
2  weeks of CHF
2-6 weeks of Yale nuclear
1-2 weeks of VA nuclear
3-4 weeks of Yale echo
3-4 weeks of VA echo
1 weeks of SRC echo
3-4 weeks of Yale cath
1-2 weeks of VA cath
1-2 weeks of SRC cath
2 weeks of research
0 weeks of CT-MRI
0 weeks of Elective
For First year fellows, the total weeks of White consults + SRC consults + VA consults = 10-12 weeks
For First year fellows, the total weeks of Yale cath + SRC cath + VA cath = 6-7 weeks
For First year fellows, the total weeks of Yale echo + SRC echo + VA echo = 8-9 weeks
For First year fellows, the total weeks of Yale nuclear + VA nuclear = 4-7 weeks
For First year fellows, 1 week of Research should be before or after 1 week vacation blocks if possible
All first year fellows must complete 1 week of yale echo, 1 week of yale cath, 1 week of white consults, 1 week of CCU, and 1 week of EP before having a Night Float rotation.
For First year fellows, 1 week of CHF should be immediately before the 1st night float rotation if possible
For First year fellows, Night float cannot come after the following rotations: white consults, SRC consults, VA consults, CCU, vacation 
No vacation in the first 2 months for first year fellows
