Call schedule rules: 

Call schedules should be on a separate calendar display (override the current tile "24-Hr Call Assignments" on the 'Generate Schedule' page)

24 hour call rules:

There is one first year fellow on 24 hour call every weekend 

24 hour call can only be given to fellows on the following rotations 

EP 

CHF 

Yale nuclear 

VA nuclear 

Yale echo 

VA echo 

SRC echo 

Yale cath 

VA cath 

SRC cath 

24 hour cannot be the week before night float 

Each first year fellow should have between 5 - 6 24 hour calls  

First year fellows should ideally be on yale echo during their first 24 hour call 

First year fellows ideally should complete one week of white consults before their first 24 hour call 

The same fellow cannot be on 24 hour call within 2 consecutive weeks 

	 

If a fellow is on white consult, CCU, SRC consult, VA consult, or night float during the week of Thanksgiving, they ideally cannot be on 24 hour call on December 24, December 25, December 31
